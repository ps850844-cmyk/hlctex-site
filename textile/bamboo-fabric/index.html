This bamboo knit fabric is made from 95% Tanboocel sourced from TANBOOCEL UNION and 5% spandex, with a weight of 200 g/m² and a width of 150 cm. It combines bamboo fibre’s natural breathability and moisture-wicking properties with the excellent elasticity of spandex, delivering a buttery-soft hand feel and four-way stretch. A waterless dyeing process reduces friction on the fibres, creating a smooth, silk-like fabric surface. It is an ideal choice for baby pajamas, zippies, swaddles and loungewear. The fabric is certified to OEKO-TEX STANDARD 100, helping ensure safety and next-to-skin comfort.

QUALITY INSPECTION

Inspection & Testing Centre

From fabric inspection to laboratory testing, HLC follows standardised quality-control procedures and supports buyer requirements based on ASTM, AATCC and JIS methods.

INSPECTION

Four-Point Fabric Inspection

Our trained inspection team records and reports defects such as holes, broken ends, skew and staining under the four-point system.

LAB

Testing Centre

Trained laboratory technicians evaluate colourfastness, pH, pilling, shrinkage and other finished-fabric performance requirements.

EQUIPMENT

Testing Equipment

X-Rite light boxes, spectrophotometers, Martindale testers, crocking testers, US-standard washers and dryers, and xenon lightfastness equipment.

02 / LAB EQUIPMENT

Testing equipment in our inspection centre

Equipment used for colour assessment, colourfastness, abrasion, pilling and dimensional-stability testing.
